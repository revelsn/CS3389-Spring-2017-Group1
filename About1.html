About Wiggly Piggly

Your Neighborhood Friendly Store

Who We Are

Wiggly Piggly is a family owned store based out of Montgomery, Alabama. We have been in operation for over 20 years.

What We're About

Our store is operated on the idea that customers should feel welcomed upon entering the store. We strive to focus on giving our customers a value for their dollar. Value would mean nothing without quality so we are constantly trying to keep the freshest parishable items along with a wide selection of grocery items. We consider ourselves fortunate to be a part of the community we serve.

How May We Serve You?

We provide a variety of services to accomodate our customer's shopping needs. Whether you choose to come shop or would like for us to do the shopping for you, we are here to serve you. We hope to see you soon!
